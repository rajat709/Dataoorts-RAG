10. Stop or Hibernate GPU Instances & Stopped Instances Pricing:
When an instance is stopped/hibernated, the charge is $0.036 per hour.
Stopping an instance immediately halts regular billing, but allows 20–25 minutes for snapshot checkpoint creation. It is recommended to wait 20–25 minutes before restarting. Public IP addresses for X-Series VMs remain reserved and unchanged even when the instance is stopped.
Note: Currently, only the X Series and Nova Series support the instance stop/hibernation feature, The hibernation feature for other series is not available yet and will be introduced in future updates.

--------------------------------------------------------------------

11. Public IP of GPU Instances:
All GPU instances are assigned a dedicated public IP address. You have complete control over IP configuration and firewall settings.
For X Series instances, the public IP remains fixed and does not change after stopping or hibernating the instance (until it is terminated).
For other series, the public IP address may change if the instance is stopped, hibernated, or deleted.

12. Windows GPU Server
Currently, DMI (Dataoorts Machine Image) supports only Linux-based operating systems. Due to compatibility and virtualization limitations with GPUs on Windows servers, we presently offer Linux environments to ensure maximum stability and performance.
Linux is straightforward and easy to use, especially for AI, machine learning, and GPU-based workloads. It also provides greater control, flexibility, and customization compared to Windows Server environments.
Windows-based GPU instances may be introduced in the future as support and compatibility improve.

13. Complete FAQs

Is there an option for Persistent Volume ?
Yes, you can create a persistent volume in a specific region and mount it to your GPU VM for continuous storage. This way, you won’t need to re-download your data each time.

In which region are Nova Instances available ?
Nova Instances are available in the Northern Europe region.

Can I restart the instance from where I left off after stopping it ?
Yes, you can. However, when you stop the instance, please allow 15–20 minutes for a snapshot checkpoint to be created. Once the snapshot is completed, you can safely restart the instance. Do not interrupt the process while the snapshot is being generated.

What is Spot GPU Instance ?
Spot GPU instances are idle resources offered at a lower cost. When demand arises, they may be reclaimed for other workloads. They’re ideal for testing or sandbox tasks, as they provide a more affordable option compared to on-demand or reserved instances.

Can I stop any GPU instance?
Yes GPU instances can be stopped. Once stopped, they incur a minimal charge of $0.036 per hour.

36. About Dataoorts DDRA
DDRA (Dynamic Distributed Resource Allocation) is the core technology behind Dataoorts that redefines how GPU resources are provisioned, shared, and scaled in the cloud.
Traditional GPU clouds rely on static allocation—users rent entire GPUs or fixed instances, often paying for idle capacity they never use. DDRA breaks this limitation by dynamically virtualizing and distributing GPU resources in real time, ensuring every fraction of compute power is used efficiently.

What Makes DDRA Different?
1. Real-time GPU Virtualization
DDRA virtualizes GPUs at runtime, allowing multiple workloads to safely and efficiently share GPU resources without performance bottlenecks.

2. Dynamic Allocation Based on Demand
Resources are allocated exactly when needed and released instantly when not in use. No pre-provisioning. No wasted compute.

3. Distributed GPU Pooling
Instead of binding workloads to a single GPU, DDRA intelligently pools and orchestrates GPUs across the infrastructure, delivering seamless scalability.

4. Pay-for-What-You-Use Efficiency
Users are billed strictly on actual GPU usage—seconds, not hours—making high-performance AI workloads accessible and cost-effective.

Why DDRA Matters:
Up to massive cost savings by eliminating idle GPU time
Instant scaling for AI inference, training, and GenAI workloads
Lower entry barrier for startups, developers, and researchers
Environmentally efficient computing through optimal resource utilization

DDRA is not just about performance—it’s about sustainable, responsible compute usage. By maximizing GPU utilization, Dataoorts reduces unnecessary energy consumption while delivering enterprise-grade performance.

Built for Modern AI & GenAI - DDRA is optimized for:
Large Language Models (LLMs)
AI inference APIs
On-demand model training
Serverless AI applications
High-throughput data processing

Whether you’re a solo developer or a scaling startup, DDRA adapts automatically to your workload—no infrastructure tuning required.
The Future of GPU Cloud - With DDRA, Dataoorts moves beyond conventional cloud computing into true serverless GPU infrastructure—where compute power becomes fluid, elastic, and instantly available.
No fixed instances. No locked resources. Just pure performance, when you need it.
